Origin Story

In 1995, we opened our flagship store in Sunnyvale. Our founder, Matthew Burrows, always carried his love for Thai cuisine. Since opening, we have proudly served the best Thai food around town, and have since launched 3 other store locations in the surrounding Bay Area.
